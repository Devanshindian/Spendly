Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, including your name and email address. We also store the expense records you add — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than supporting the operation of Spendly. We do not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
